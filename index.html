F.A.Q

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! Our mobile app is available on both iOS and Android. Download it from the App Store or Google Play and enjoy all features seamlessly on your smartphone.

How accurate is the voice-to-text conversion?

Our voice-to-text conversion is highly accurate, utilizing advanced AI algorithms to ensure reliable transcription in various environments and accents.

Can I use TalkNotes in different languages?

Yes! TalkNotes supports multiple languages, allowing you to create notes in your preferred language with high accuracy.

Can I upload my own audio files?

Yes! You can upload your own audio files and have them transcribed by our voice-to-text conversion feature.

Can I edit the text once it's converted?

Yes! You can edit the transcribed text at any time to correct errors or make improvements.

Do I need an internet connection to use TalkNotes?

An internet connection is required for the voice-to-text conversion feature, as it relies on cloud-based AI processing. However, you can still access and edit your notes offline once they are transcribed.

What about privacy?

Your privacy is important to us. All audio files and transcribed text are securely stored and never shared with third parties.

How can I contact support if I have questions or need help?

You can contact our support team at support@talknotes.com or through the "Help" section within the app.

How can I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, go to your account settings and navigate to the "Billing" section. From there, you can manage your subscription and choose to cancel at any time.

How long is the discount valid when I subscribe?

The discount is valid for the first billing cycle of your subscription. After that, the standard pricing will apply.
